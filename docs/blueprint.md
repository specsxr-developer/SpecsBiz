# **App Name**: SpecsBiz | Smart Business Manager

## Core Features:

- Dashboard: Provides a quick overview of business performance with real-time summary cards, recent activities, and landing animations.
- Inventory Management: Enables adding, editing, and deleting products with features like AI-powered data perfecting, barcode scanning, and stock alerts.
- Sales Management: Allows quick sale recording, product selection, and price management with sales history and revenue tracking.
- Customer Management: Maintains a customer database with AI-driven data enhancement, segmentation, contact management, and purchase history.
- Reports & Analytics: Generates sales charts and revenue analytics, providing top product insights and period-based reports to understand business trends.
- AI Assistant: Integrates OpenAI for smart data entry, business insights, and offers an AI chat assistant, supporting multilingual interactions and acts as a tool that the user can customize with API keys.
- Offline/Online Sync: Utilizes a local SQLite database for offline functionality and automatically synchronizes data when online, resolving conflicts and backing up data.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#191970) for headers, providing a professional and sophisticated feel, taken from the original image.
- Background color: Very light cyan (#E0FFFF) providing a calm and clean background to maintain focus on the data.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) to highlight key interactive elements and call-to-action buttons.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean and modern look that ensures readability across the app, well suited to extended blocks of text as well as titles.
- Use minimalist, modern icons that clearly represent different functionalities within the app. These icons will have a teal accent.
- Employ a card-based layout with smooth animations for an interactive and engaging user experience.
- Subtle animations to provide feedback on user interactions and guide the user through the app.
